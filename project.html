    Yan Li

    • SELECTED PROJECT: RUN WITH THE DREAM •

    Social Context

    In recent years, O2O has developed rapidly in China and has entered a mature market in just a few years.
    However, this makes a group with a helmet, wearing the uniforms of the rider had to face the social contradictions in the streets and lanes shuttle.

    Design Origin

    During their leisure time, they hardly can find a place to rest in the downtown area without disturbing others because of their uniform which represents their lower social status. Therefore, this design aim to create a pocket park near Restaurants for riders relax and networking to balance social justice.

    This plaza is a chance that could:

    • Trigger them talking and connection with others
    • Feel sense of belonging and cared
    • Help them identify their reasonable existence in the city

    Urban Furniture Improvement

    I add foldable seats and imrpove the parking structure on the site.I hope that it could expel them from worrying about their motorcycle when they leaving; and also make this area more organized.

    Epilogue

    To some extent, delivery riders are confronted with the collective anxiety of Chinese in contemporary society: how to achieve the goals safer in a shorter time? It is simply that the goals vary with the individual.
    Delivery riders just want to deliver the food quickly and safely; the others may thirst for something MUCH FURTHER AND MORE.
